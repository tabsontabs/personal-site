Projects

  • The Villager Finder

    An app that allows you to filter through characters from Animal Crossing: New Horizons. Built using React and the ACNH Api.

  • It's Wine Time

    A quiz app that provides you with an LCBO wine recommendation based on your selected preferences. Built using jQuery featuring wine recos from my very own little wine blog.

  • The Gallery Project

    An app that I pair programmed with another amazing developer, Kat DeVrieze. We used jQuery and the Met Museum API. The app allows you to view artworks and relevant information based on a search query.

  • My Watchlist

    An app that I mob programmed with these awesome developers - Shonda Moshis, Lawrence Hebia, and Jerry Dong. We used React, Firebase, and the TVmaze Api.

    The app allows you to search for TV shows and relevant information. You can also create your own "watchlists", add TV shows to your lists, and rate TV shows within your lists.
